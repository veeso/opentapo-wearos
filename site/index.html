OpenTapo

A WearOs app to control your TP-Link Tapo devices

Google Play GitHub Ko-Fi

Features:

  • Automatically discovers the devices present on your local WiFi network
  • Control all your TAPO devices
  • Set power state
  • Set lamp brightness
  • Set lamp color
  • Group your devices
